Director's Message

“ Do NOT train children to learn by force and harshness,
but direct them to do it by what amuses their minds,
so that you may be better able to discover with accuracy
the peculiar bent of the genius of each. ” -Plato-

Welcome to Seodaemun POLY!!

I would like to welcome all visitors, parents, and students to Seodaemun campus website.
Motivation is the key to learning and teaching. I believe depends on good teachers, they can inspire passion for learning in their students.
However, even if good teachers motivate them in classes, we cannot plan for effective instruction until we know where they are, where you want them to go, and how you want to get there. For that reason, POLY learning curriculums and POLY Language Amplitude Test (PLAT) will help our motto of giving effective planning, teaching, and learning. PLAT will give students and parents a profile or report on student achievement. And with student input and ownership, assessment can even motivate students to perform and produce.

Thank you for taking your valuable time to visit our website. As the director of Seodaemun POLY, I am ensured that all of our teachers and staffs are dedicated to give your child the greatest learning experience possible.
Sincerely,

Annie Eunhee Cho
